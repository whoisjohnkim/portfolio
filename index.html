About Me

My name is John Hyunmin Kim and I graduated from the University of Michigan with a degree in Biophysics and a minor in Computer Science. Throughout my years of working as a research assistant in a Neurology lab at the University of Michigan, I was able to cultivate my love for problem solving and developed my ability to create clean and efficient protocols.

I am now an aspiring web developer and currently enrolled in the University of Texas' Web development Coding Bootcamp. I have experience with algorithms and data structures through C++ courses at the University of Michigan, along with Web Development technologies including CSS, HTML, Python, and Javascript.
